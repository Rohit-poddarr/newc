Refund and Cancellation Policy :

How To Apply for Fee Refund in Offline Courses

1. Last Date to Apply For First Refund and Last Refund is mentioned on relevant Course Page of this Course Booklet and in your Fee Challan Fee receipt. Amount Deductible in First Refund Case and Last Refund Case (towards Administrative & Academic Expenses) are also mentioned in the relevant Course Page of this Course Booklet/our website and Fee Challan.

2. Prescribed Refund Application Form can be obtained from Admission Office and can also be downloaded from our website.

3. Refund request made verbally or through phone/email/fax shall not be entertained in any case.

4. Refund Application in the prescribed format will be accepted along with Original Fee Receipt, BEYOND EQUATIONS Identify card & Attendance Card, No refund will be made after the Last Refund Date, except special cases as below.

5. Refund will be credited in Father/Mother/Student Bank Account through NEFT/RTGS mode only. For this you have to submit Cancelled Cheque or Bank Passbook first page copy along with Refund Form.

6. Refund rules are SAME even a. If a student joins class after commencement date or b. If a student doesnâ€™t join or attend even a single class at all or c. If a student apply for refund before commencement of the class.

Refund in Special Case After Last Refund Date

1. If an enrolled Student get Admission in any Medical College (for which he has taken Admission) located in India for MBBS/BDS. only through any Competitive Examination, refund will be made after deducting amout equivalent to Last Refund amount out of free paid. Refund Shall not be payable if amount paid is less than amount equivalent to Last Refund amount.

2. Refund of fee will be applicable only for the aforesaid courses. Refund will not be made for B.Pharma/B.YNS/B. Physiotherapy/B.VSc, B.Sc. and other Courses.

3. Last date for such special case refund is 30/09/2023.

4. For this following doucments must be submitted along with prescribed Refund Application Form. a. Original Fee Receipt of BEYOND EQUATIONS. b. Identify Card of BEYOND EQUATIONS. c. Attendance Card of BEYOND EQUATIONS. d. Photo Copy of College Call Letter/Counseling Letter. e. Fee Receipt of College Admission.

5. Refund application on the ground of severe disease may be considered by the management committee, after passing Last Refund date on submission of Medical Prescription/Report.

How To Apply for Fee Refund in Online Courses

No Refund will be applicable in any of Beyond Equations online courses.
